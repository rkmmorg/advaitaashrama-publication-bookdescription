

This volume contains rare spiritual counsels given by the direct disciples of Sri Ramakrishna to spiritual aspirants.These records derive their value from the simplicity and directness with which they touch upon and illuminate many aspects of practical religion, and will be a mine of spiritual guidance to all spiritual aspirants. The book contains the spiritual talks of the Holy Mother, of Swamis Brahmananda, Premananda, Turiyananda, Shivananda, and Saradananda.
