The inspirational teachings collected in this book, selected from the writings and talks of Swami Vivekananda, speak directly to the concerns of contemporary men and women who seek to live a spiritual life in the midst of everyday activities. At the same time, they form a concise introduction to Vedanta, the perennial philosophy of India. This book brings us in touch with the source, the words of the great Swami and his central message. Herein lies the book's special value.
