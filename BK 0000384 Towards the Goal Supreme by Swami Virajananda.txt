This book is an inspiring collection of letters written by Swami Virajananda in response to the many questions on meditation and spiritual life from his disciples. They contain practical hints, penetrating insights and profound counsels for all spiritual aspirants.They bring the reader in personal contact with a great teacher who has actually experienced what he teaches.

Swami Virajananda was a disciple of Swami Vivekananda and President of the Ramakrishna Order (1938-1951).
