







This booklet comprises the text of the speeches delivered by Jawaharlal Nehru the first Prime Minister of India on Sri Ramakrishna and Swami Vivekananda on two different occasions. Since the dawn of these two spiritual luminaries on the horizon of modern India they have evoked a deep interest in the subject of religion and spirituality among a wide circle of people so as to include intellectuals philosophers scientists politicians statesmen and others to mention just a few. In this light it would be extremely interesting for all the admirers and devotees of Sri Ramakrishna and Swami Vivekananda to know what the well-known statesman of India had to say on them.
