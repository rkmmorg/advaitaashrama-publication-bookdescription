This wonderful book gives the readers a vivid picture of one of Sri Ramakrishna's great disciples whose colourful and profoundly influential life has heretofore been little known. The author, besides describing the life of Swami Trigunatita in India gives a detailed account of his pioneering work in the West in nurturing the seeds of Vedanta Movement sown there by Swami Vivekananda. This book is must for everyone interested in the Ramakrishna-Vivekananda Movement.
