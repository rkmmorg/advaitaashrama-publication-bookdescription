

This volume contains the translations of the following four Upanishads (Aitareya, Mundaka, Mandukya (along with Gaudapada's Karika), and Prashna and its commentaries by Sri Shankara. Like volume 1, it contains the text of the Upanishads in Devanagari, a lucid and faithful translation of the text and commentary, relevant notes, reference to quotations, and index to texts.
