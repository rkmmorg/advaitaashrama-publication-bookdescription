

These Upanishads though called minor in order to distinguish them from the ten well-known ones commented on by Sri Shankaracharya are nonetheless very important. The volume contains Paramahamsa Atma Amritabindu Tejabindu Sarva Brahma Aruneyi and Kaivalya Upanishads. With an informative introduction to the book and original text in Devanagari followed by its English rendering and copious notes this book will be extremely valuable to all the students of Vedanta and seekers after Truth.
