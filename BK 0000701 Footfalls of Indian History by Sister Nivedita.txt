Here the author in her inimitable style gives a glimpse of the past glories and drawbacks of India in a nutshell. She has discussed the most important topics regarding Religion Philosophy Culture Economics Architecture influence of the Gupta Dynasty historical significance of the northern pilgrimage and some problems of Indian research. In the last chapter she has drawn a fine picture of Varanasi the most ancient city of India. This is a book that will help all those who are eager to learn more about India.
